# Project "None" - Branding Guide

## 1. Project Overview
*   **Project Name**: None
*   **Slogan**: Reclaim Your Focus.
*   **Mission**: To empower individuals and teams to minimize digital distractions, cultivate deep focus, and achieve greater well-being and productivity through intentional digital interaction.
*   **Core Values**: Minimalism, Focus, Clarity, Intentionality, Empowerment.

## 2. Logo Concepts

### Concept A: The Empty Circle
*   **Description**: A clean, minimalist circle, perhaps with a slight break or a subtle gradient effect, symbolizing a clear space, an absence of clutter, or a moment of pause. The "N" of None could be subtly integrated or placed within/around the circle.
*   **Meaning**: Represents completeness, wholeness, but also emptiness in a positive sense (no distractions). The break could symbolize breaking free from digital chains.
