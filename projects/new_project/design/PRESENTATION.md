### **2. Introducing "None" - Our Solution**
*   **Slide Title**: None: Your Gateway to Digital Serenity
*   **Content**:
    *   **What is None?**: A comprehensive digital minimalism platform designed to help users consciously reduce digital clutter, block distractions, and cultivate deep focus.
    *   **Core Philosophy**: It's not about disconnecting entirely, but about intentional engagement and creating space for what truly matters.
    *   Brief overview of the main components (e.g., smart blocking, focus modes, insights).

---

### **3. Key Features**
*   **Slide Title**: How None Empowers Focus
*   **Content**:
    *   **Intelligent Blocker**:
        *   Customizable website and application blocking based on user-defined focus modes (e.g., "Deep Work," "Family Time," "Sleep").
        *   Time-based blocking schedules and one-click activation.
    *   **Notification Silencer**:
        *   Advanced control over notifications across devices, allowing only critical alerts during focus periods.
        *   "Do Not Disturb" scheduler with exceptions.
    *   **Digital Detox Scheduler**:
        *   Pre-plan periods of complete digital disconnection for rejuvenation.
        *   "Emergency override" with friction to discourage impulsive breaking.
    *   **Usage Insights & Analytics**:
        *   Personalized reports on app usage, distraction patterns, and focus duration.
        *   Visualizations to help users understand their digital habits and identify areas for improvement.
    *   **Focus Modes & Presets**:
        *   Create and save custom profiles for different activities (e.g., "Writing Mode," "Study Mode," "Mindfulness Mode").
        *   Integrations with calendar and to-do apps for context-aware mode switching.
